Every Town is an blank canvas and you are the artist. The rarity of the Town determines its maximum upgradeable size. Upgrading your Town’s launchpad allows you to host more mints per week. Any upgrades or changes you make to your Town are saved on-chain so its value and progress are never deleted, even if ownership is transferred.

Getting started with DropTown

It’s quite simple really…



  1. Create a DropTown account and have a Gen0 Citizen + Town NFT
  2. Access the Town Square dashboard with your Citizen NFT
  3. Select your Town NFT and begin creating your website with our drag-and-drop web editor complete with NFT launchpad
  4. Connect with projects and onboard them and their assets to schedule their minting date (or launch your own NFTs)
  5. Host the mint and earn on the profits
  6. Establish your own branded NFT business
