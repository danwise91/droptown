100% User Owned

Through the power of NFTs, all that’s needed to get started is a Citizen and Town NFT. Here are the important roles each collection plays…


Citizen NFTs Access to the Town Square dashboard

Town NFTs Used to store metadata pertaining to a created website


Your Town NFT IS your business, meaning, you can sell or trade it if you ever wish to. Essentially, DropTown enables you to buy and sell e-businesses through the power of NFTs. That means if you’ve built your site into a profitable business it could constitute a higher asking price!

Getting started with DropTown

It’s quite simple really…



  1. Create a DropTown account and have a Gen0 Citizen + Town NFT
  2. Access the Town Square dashboard with your Citizen NFT
  3. Select your Town NFT and begin creating your website with our drag-and-drop web editor complete with NFT launchpad
  4. Connect with projects and onboard them and their assets to schedule their minting date (or launch your own NFTs!)
  5. Host the mint and earn on the profits
  6. Establish your own branded NFT business

NFT Drops
Drop #1 - Gen0 Citizen NFTs
Total supply: 5438

Our first NFT drop will be the Gen0 Citizen NFTs. Your citizen NFT is your unique identity in DropTown and comes with plenty of valuable perks such as access to our Town NFT sale and lifetime discounts on all marketplace items. Having even 1 citizen NFT guarantees you 1 Town plot.

Drop #2 Towns
Total supply 5438

Every Town is an blank canvas and you are the artist. The rarity of the Town determines its maximum upgradeable size. Upgrading your Town’s launchpad allows you to host more mints per week. Any upgrades or changes you make to your Town are saved on-chain so its value and progress are never deleted, even if ownership is transferred.
