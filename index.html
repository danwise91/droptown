The Town Square

Access the Town Square, the main dashboard providing you with all the tools you need to get your launchpad up and running. Build your launchpad website with our drag-and-drop editor, making it easier than ever to start hosting NFT mints.

Spend $DROP tokens to unlock additional features and ways to expand your launchpad’s services. Stake your citizen NFTs to passively earn $DROP, connect with projects looking for launchpads, view upcoming minting schedules, basically everything you need is in the square!

Build a brand

Owning a launchpad is an opportunity to build a brand. DropTown provides ways for you to re-invest your hard-earned $DROP tokens so you can stand out from the rest.

Upload designs and customize your launchpad to shape it in your image with our user-friendly interface. DropTown also provides ways for you to promote your brand utilizing DropTown channels.
